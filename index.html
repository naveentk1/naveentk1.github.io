The Future of Multimodal AI
September 30, 2025
The convergence of vision, audio, and language models is a frontier of AI. As we've seen with Oesepheus, the goal isn't always bigger models, but smarter, more efficient ones. The ability to process multiple data types simultaneously on edge devices is a game-changer for privacy and accessibility. We're moving towards a world where AI can understand context in a much more human-like way, interpreting spoken commands, visual cues, and text all at once.
A Quick Look at High-Frequency Trading
September 25, 2025
High-frequency trading (HFT) is more than just speed. It's about leveraging market microstructure and predictive models to capitalize on fleeting opportunities. Our work with Trading Systems focuses on developing robust, data-driven strategies that can navigate the volatility and complexity of modern markets. It's a field where a millisecond can make all the difference.
