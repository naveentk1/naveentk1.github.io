# Oesepheus
Oesepheus is a 1B parameter Visual-Audio-Speech Model that represents our vision for accessible multimodal AI.
While the industry pushes toward ever-larger models, we took the opposite approach: building a unified VASM
that processes vision, audio, and language on an 8GB M1 MacBook Air. Through novel cross-modal fusion
architectures and extreme optimization, Oesepheus proves that sophisticated multimodal understanding can run
locally, privately, and efficiently—bringing powerful AI capabilities directly to edge devices without cloud dependence.
